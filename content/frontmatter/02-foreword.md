
# Foreword to the Urdu Edition {.unnumbered}

::: {custom-style="basmalah"}

In the Name of Allāh, the Most Compassionate, the Most Merciful,

There is a genuine lack of sound educational literature in Urdu for
Muslim children. This gap made it necessary to produce a concise yet
comprehensive work---one that gathers the essential teachings of Islam
directly from the Noble Quran and the authentic Sunnah in a clear and
orderly manner.

The distinction of this book lies in the fact that it presents those
teachings which every Muslim must know: matters related to belief
(ʿaqīdah), worship (ʿibādāt), social conduct and transactions
(muʿāmalāt), and moral character (akhlāq). These teachings have been
carefully drawn from the āyahs of the Quran and the sound Prophetic
traditions, and then explained in a simple, engaging style suited to the
understanding of children. Alongside this, key issues of creed and
jurisprudence have been clarified in an accessible and meaningful way.

What further lends strength to this work is that Quranic teachings are
consistently supported by authentic Prophetic narrations, and topics of
belief and law are not treated superficially but explained with due care
and clarity. It is hoped that this book will prove beneficial not only
for children but for all those who seek to derive guidance from it.

This modest effort was completed under the supervision of Maulānā
Muḥammad Idrīs al-Kāndhlawī, may Allāh elevate his rank. It was
subsequently reviewed at Nadwat al-ʿUlamāʾ, and thereafter examined by
Mawlānā Ghāzī Rashīd Aḥmad, may Allāh have mercy upon him.

It is our hope that this work will help draw the attention of Muslims
toward the proper religious upbringing of their children, and that Allāh
will make it a means of lasting benefit.
