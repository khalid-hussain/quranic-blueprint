In the Name of Allāh, the Most Compassionate, the Most Merciful

The book in hand, *The Quranic Blueprint*, is a faithful English
translation of the renowned work *Taʿlīm al-Quran*, authored several
decades ago by the eminent Islamic scholar of his era, the late
Professor Shaykh Owais al-Nagrāmī al-Nadwī \hon{\M_HIM}{\BODY} of Dār
al-ʿUlūm Nadwat al-ʿUlamāʾ, Lucknow. Shaykh Owais al-Nadwī [رحمه
الله]{dir="rtl"} was a towering intellectual figure whose contributions
to Quranic studies, tafsīr, and Islamic thought were widely recognised
and deeply respected in scholarly circles across the subcontinent and
beyond.

Upon its original publication, *Taʿlīm al-Quran* was received with
great appreciation by scholars and lay readers alike. Its enduring
relevance lies in its authentic sourcing, clarity of thought, and the
author's ability to present core Islamic beliefs and Quranic guidance
in a style that is at once profound and accessible. Decades later, the
work continues to speak meaningfully to readers navigating the moral,
intellectual, and spiritual challenges of modern life. It is for this
reason that the need to translate this important work into the English
language was long felt and deeply necessary.

The present English translation, undertaken by the Shaykh's
great-grandson, Mohammad Zahid ibn Tariq, is a valuable and timely
contribution to the growing body of reliable Islamic literature
available to English-speaking audiences. In an age where social media
and the internet are saturated with misinformation, half-truths, and
unauthenticated material about Islam and Muslims, the availability of a
sound, well-referenced, and methodologically grounded work such as this
is of immense importance---particularly for Muslim youth, university
students, and thoughtful readers seeking clarity and guidance.

This book offers comprehensive direction for leading a righteous and
balanced life in today's increasingly materialistic society. It
addresses nearly every sphere in which guidance is required: from sound
Islamic beliefs to lawful and unlawful matters, personal conduct, social
dealings, ethics, morality, and spiritual refinement. Shaykh Owais
al-Nadwī \hon{\M_HIM}{\BODY} supports his discussions with relevant
Quranic āyahs, authentic aḥādīth, and references from leading scholars
of the Ummah, elaborating upon them in a manner that makes even complex
concepts easily understandable for the ordinary reader. In this sense,
the book serves not only as a work of tafsīr-inspired guidance but also
as a practical handbook of Islam for both youth and laypersons.

One of the most remarkable features of *Taʿlīm al-Quran* is the
author's ability to cover such a wide range of essential topics with
precision and depth, yet within a concise and readable framework. This
quality greatly enhances its accessibility and appeal. We are confident
that this English translation will be warmly received and widely
benefited from, and we hope that it will, in time, be translated into
other languages as well.

We commend Mohammad Zahid ibn Tariq for rendering this authentic and
comprehensive work into English with sincerity and care, and for
preserving the works of a family with a rich scholarly heritage. His
efforts represent a meaningful service to the Ummah, particularly to
those who earnestly wish to understand Islam through sound scholarship.
We pray that Allah \hon{\SWT}{\BODY} accepts this noble endeavour, places
barakah in it, and grants success in both this world and the Hereafter
to all who contributed to its completion in any capacity. Āmīn.
